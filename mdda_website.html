Our Story

Empowering Rural Voices on a Global Stage

Majestic Discourse Debate Academy (MDDA) is a South African nonprofit that provides structured debate training to learners from under-resourced schools — at zero cost to schools or families.

Empowerment

We unlock confidence, critical thought, and the courage to speak in every learner we work with.

Equity

Access to debate should never be determined by geography or income. We actively level the field.

Excellence

We hold our learners to the same standards as the best debaters in the world — because we know they can meet them.

Our Programmes

Everything Your Learners Need to Win

MDDA provides a complete debate development pathway — from first workshop to international tournament — at no cost to schools or families.

🎤

Expert Debate Coaching

Structured coaching in argument construction, rebuttals, public speaking, and point of information — delivered by experienced coaches who have competed nationally and internationally.

🏟️

Tournament Preparation

Full preparation for provincial, national, and international tournaments. We handle registration, motions preparation, and mock debates so your learners step onto the stage ready.

🧠

Critical Thinking Development

We don't just teach debate — we teach learners how to think. Our curriculum develops research skills, logical reasoning, and the ability to engage confidently with complex ideas.

🤝

Mentorship & Community

Learners join a network of debaters across South Africa. Our mentorship programme connects them with alumni, coaches, and university students who have walked the same journey.

📚

Curriculum Resources

Schools receive structured curriculum materials, debate guides, and motion banks that teachers and learners can use independently between coaching sessions.

🌐

Global Exposure

Top performing learners are connected to international debate opportunities, exposing them to global perspectives and creating pathways to universities and opportunities worldwide.

The Pathway

Your Learners' Journey With MDDA

From the first session to the national stage — here is what the journey looks like.

1

School Partnership

Your school signs a simple partnership agreement with MDDA. No fees. No lengthy onboarding. Just a commitment to make space for debate.

2

Introductory Workshops

MDDA coaches visit your school to run introductory sessions. We identify learners with potential and build their foundation from day one.

3

Regular Training

Ongoing weekly or fortnightly sessions, structured around national tournament calendars and learner development milestones.

4

Tournament & Beyond

Your learners compete. They win. They grow. And the best go on to international tournaments, university debates, and leadership roles.

Debate Formats

We Train in the World's Leading Formats

Our learners are prepared to compete in the same formats used at the world's top debate tournaments.

Format 1

British Parliamentary (BP)

The world's most widely used debate format, used at WUDC and most South African university tournaments. Teams of two debate motions across government and opposition benches.

Format 2

World Schools Debate (WSD)

The format used at the World Schools Debating Championships. Combines prepared and impromptu motions — the gold standard for secondary school debate worldwide.

Format 3

World Scholars Cup (WSC)

A global interdisciplinary debate programme connecting learners across the world. MDDA prepares learners for WSC regional, national, and international rounds.
